Why Olhão ?

The Shellfish Capital

The municipal markets of Olhão, one of the ex-libris of the town of Olhão, began to be built in 1912 and were inaugurated four years later. For nearly a century, they have been one of the illustrated postcards of Olhão and a must for tourists and residents.

The historic quarter is one of the most important places to be seen and visited in Olhão because of its typicity and history. The cubist architecture is unique in the country.

Figo de Pita

Serves meat, vegan options available. Health food restaurant offering vegan raw spring rolls, fried sweet potatoes, burgers, smoothies and a variety of tofu bowls. Open Tue-Sat 09:30-17:00, Sun 09:30-15:00.

Address

R.Moinho da Baretta 48, 8700-431 Olhão

What I like about it

It is a really cosy place with a good vibe. The meals are very colourful and more importantly, delicious! It is great for meat eaters but also for vegan eaters. I just love it.
